Making The Invisible Visible

Visualising Air Quality In An Understandable Way

03/2014 - 09/2014
#VISUALIZATION #MOBILE
People are not aware of the severity of pollution issues in cities. Because not only pollutants are invisible, the health effects are hard to be linked to specific human behaviours. The InstaNO2 mobile app proposed a photo-based air quality visual representation to visualise air quality data. The app allowed people to explore the current NO2 level from places they were via an existing habitual behaviour, taking photos. Using photograph as a medium, the app visualised air quality data by displaying the numeric air quality data on top of the photograph with a green or red, healthy or unhealthy colour block, and by adjusting the appearance of the photo based on the data.
