Icebreaker

Start conversations with topics

Feel difficult to start a conversation? Don't know what topics to talk about when you first meet someone? While people find it easier to talk if they have a topic and have common interests, thus, the Icebreaker app allows users to list topics and their interests that they would like to talk about on their profiles. More importantly, the first sentence which starts the conversation would be based on the topic you choose to discuss, and from the other person, which makes it easier for you to talk.
